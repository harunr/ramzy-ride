What is Ramzy Ride

Ramzy Ride is a distinctly African ride-sharing alternative to traditional taxi cabs. Ramzy Ride uses both Android and iOS phone app to connect riders with drivers using their phone’s GPS capabilities, letting both parties know one another’s location and removing the question of when the ride will actually arrive.

Ramzy Ride seeks to enrich and empower its driver community by allowing them to share in a pool of quarterly dividends paid out of the companies profits.

Why ride with Ramzy

Convenient

One tap and a car comes directly to you. Hop in—your driver knows exactly where to go. And when you get there, just step out. Payment is completely seamless.

Safe and secure

Daily commute. Errand across town. Early morning flight. Late night drinks. Wherever you’re headed, count on Ramzy Ride for a ride—no reservations required.

Proudly South African

Economy cars at everyday prices are always available. For special occasions, no occasion at all, or when you just a need a bit more room, call a black car or SUV.
